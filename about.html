About Paws Delights

We’re a small team of dog lovers crafting safe, homemade-style treats for pups with sensitive stomachs.

Our Story

It started with one tiny dog who needed gentle snacks. We began baking in small batches, focusing on clean ingredients and textures that are easy to chew.

Today we still make every recipe with the same care, so you can feel good about every treat you share.

How We Work

• Short ingredient lists you can read aloud.

• Batch testing to keep quality high and flavors consistent.

• Designed for small breeds first—sizes and densities are gentle.

Our Values

Safety

No artificial colors or preservatives. We avoid common triggers and keep recipes simple.

Transparency

Clear labels and honest sourcing. Ask us anything—we love sharing how each treat is made.

Joy

Treat time should feel special. We bake for aroma, texture, and tail-wagging excitement.

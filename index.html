FAQ

Are the treats safe for small dogs? Yes. We design portion sizes and textures for small breeds and use gentle ingredients.
Do you ship nationwide? We currently ship across Korea. Reach out if you need special delivery options.
How should I store the treats? Keep sealed in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate softer treats for extra freshness.
Do you use common allergens? We avoid artificial additives and limit common triggers; check each flavor for specifics.
Can I use these for training? Absolutely. Most treats break cleanly into small pieces for quick rewards.
Will there be more flavors? We love experimenting. Join our email updates to be first to taste new recipes.
